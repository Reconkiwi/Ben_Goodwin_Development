ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Ben Goodwin. I am a 21 year old student developer studying at the University of Winchester. Although my course is mainly focusssed on web development, I spend a lot of my time experimenting with the development of AR, VR and MR applications as well as various 2D and 3D game projects.

Below are some examples of some of my favorite projects that I have compiled over the last three years of University. Let me know what you think!

PORTFOLIO

The LUFC Site - Y3S1

Dec 24, 2018

The LUFC News project was a rebrand of Alex Matthews’ fan made community/ social media group and website. I had a direct role in re-developing Alex’s website by creating a new Wordpress theme for him from scratch which included a login system and live chat room.

The LUFC News App

Dec 24, 2018

The LUFC News App was a follow on from the LUFC Site where I was asked to develop a prototype that included login systems and post data directly from the site itself.

Arkitekt.UI

Dec 24, 2018

Francis, a masters student in my faculty proposed to me an AR app prototype that needed building. Arkitekt.UI is an architectural client based application which aims to allow the client to have a consistent impact on the development and design of their building. It is built using Apple’s new Quick View AR and Google’s Firebase Database service.

AR Lost Cat Poster

Dec 24, 2018

A masters student in my faculty came to me with a prototype proposal that they needed building towards their final submission. Maisie wanted me to create an AR missing cat poster application. The app was developed using AR.JS and then wrapped using ionic.

Mixed Reality Tower Defence - Y3S2

The mixed reality tower defence was a prototype built using Unity3D for Microsoft's HoloLens. The prototype included two tower types, voice recognition, hand gestures and editable waves.

The LUFC News Site - Y3S1

The LUFC News project was a rebrand of Alex Matthews' fan made community social media group and website. I had a direct role in re-developing Alex's website by creating a new Wordpress theme for him from scratch which included a login system and live chat room.

The LUFC News App - Y3S1

AR Lost Cat Poster - Y3S1

Arkitekt.UI - Y3S1

Francis, a masters student in my faculty proposed to me an AR app prototype that needed building. Arkitekt.UI is an architectural client based application which aims to allow the client to have a consistent impact on the development and design of their building. It is built using Apple's new Quick View AR and Google's Firebase Database service.

HeartBeat MR - Y2S2

The Silk Mill - Y2S2

For our client projects this semester, we were given a series of briefs to complete in groups. My group and I opted to take on Whitchurch Silk Mill's brief which was to develop a way of tracking and evaluating the visitors that entered the mill as well as monitor the popularity of their new exhibits.
